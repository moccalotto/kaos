Powers (Alphabetical order)

List of all powers in alphabetical order. Click here for a list of powers ordered by power-skill and tier.

Animal Essense

Witchcraft2

You gain any attribute of the animal that is greater than yours for 10 minutes.


Animal Weapon

Witchcraft3

You gain the claws, talons, punces, tail or even head (bite) of an animal. Your unarmed combat damage is replaced by the one of the animal for 10 minutes.


Babel

Thaumaturgy3, Witchcraft3

You can understand the meaning of any spoken or written language. You can even find some meaning in animal growls.


Berserk

Battle3

You can take two additional Surge Actions this round (they each cause normal strain damage).


Binding Handshake

Ritual: 1 minute, Duration: 1 year

Leadership2, Thaumaturgy2, Witchcraft2

The willing subject swears an oath while you draw a small symbol on some part of their body. If they break their oath within a year they suffer 10d10 points of damage.


Break

Ritual: 1 minute, Permanent

Thaumaturgy1, Witchcraft2

Touched non-living and non-magical object weighing no more than 100kg breaks in half.


Calm

Leadership1, Witchcraft2

You calm an enraged or frightend subject. If subject is magically frightened or enraged, they have to succeed an Easy WIL Test in order to counter that spell.


Confusion

Witchcraft4

If subject fails a WIL test, all attack tests made by the subject become harder. The effect lasts as long as you concentrate.


Control Athryss

Leadership3, Thaumaturgy2, Witchcraft2

You control a single Athryss creature within 20 squares.


Crane

Thaumaturgy2, Witchcraft3

Touched object (of max 200kg) can fly (at Movement Rate 6) and carry a load of up to 250kg.


Create Water

Thaumaturgy1

Water trickles slowly from an elemental rift in your closed fist.


Cure Disease

Witchcraft2

Touched subject is cured of their diseases and infections.


Dark Vision

Thaumaturgy1, Witchcraft1

You can see in complete darkness as though it was twilight.


Dark Vision, Other

Witchcraft2

Defense Attunement, Moderate

The subject regains 2d4 Defense Points.


Defensive Stance

Battle2

You regain all lost DPs.


Destroy Undead

Thaumaturgy3

All undead creatures in sight suffer 8d6 points of damage.


Detect Lies

Witchcraft1

You know when people tell you lies.


Detect Magic

Drawstrike

Battle3

You draw your melee weapon and strike your opponent in one smooth motion. You deal normal weapon damage. You do not need to make a normal attack roll.


Drill

Range: touch

Thaumaturgy1, Witchcraft2

Create a hole in wood, stone or metal. The hole can be up to a meter in length and a few centimeters thick.


Dual Strike

Battle3

You attack with two melee weapons at the same time. Both your weapons deal normal damage. No attack rolls are necessary.


Durable Rope

Ritual: 15 minutes, Permanent

Thaumaturgy3, Witchcraft4

Makes a rope 10 times stronger than it was, but reduce it to half its original length.


Enchanted Contract

Ritual: 5 minutes

Thaumaturgy3, Witchcraft3

You inscribe a pre-drawn contract with magical runes. All parties then sign the contract and you put a final rune upon it. If at any point any party willingly act in a manner that directly counter the spirit of the contract, they will suffer 10d10 points of damage. Although only once per day. Physically destroying the contract will cancel the spell.


Fear

Battle3, Leadership4, Witchcraft3

The subject must move at least 1 square away from you on their next turn. If they do not, all tests made by them that turn will become harder.


Fireball

Thaumaturgy4, Witchcraft6

You create an explosion within 10 squares that affect 9 squares (3x3 squares). Subjects within the area can avoid damage (4d6) with an AGI Test.


Fireball, Geater

Thaumaturgy5, Witchcraft6

Create an explosion within 20 squares that affect 13 squares (3 square diameter). Subjects within the area can avoid damage (6d6) with an AGI Test.


Firebolt

Thaumaturgy3, Witchcraft5

Ranged attack against subject, who can avoid damage (4d6) with an AGI Test.

You create a ball of reddish fire the size of your fist, swirling between your hands. With a throwing motion you send the ball towards your opponent, engulfing them in flames.


Fly

Thaumaturgy3, Witchcraft4

Subject can fly (Movement Rate 6) for 5 minutes.


Friend

Witchcraft4

Subject considers you a friend for the next 8 hours. Any violent action towards the subject will cancel the spell.


Friend Doll

Ritual: 30 minutes

Witchcraft2

You create a small doll whose materials include hair, nail clippings or similar from the subject. As long as you hold he doll in your hands, the subject considers you a friend. Letting go of the doll will end the spell. Any violent action towards the subject will cause the doll to burst into flame and you suffer 2d6 points of damage.


Get Up Soldier

Leadership3, Witchcraft3

Touched ally with negative HP wakes up and is able to stay conscious for WIL minutes. While in this state, all tests are made harder. Subject dies if they reach -30 HP.

Ritual: 15 minutes

Witchcraft4

Witchcraft3

Heat Mineral

Thaumaturgy2

You heat a single metal or stone object to extreme temperatures. The object will stay at that temperature as long as you concentrate on the spell. Anyone wearing the targeted mineral will suffer 2d6 points of damage per round.


Heat Tolerance

Ritual: 5 minutes

Thaumaturgy2

The subject gains Damage Reduction 20 against heat and fire damage.


Hint

Leadership5

Hold Breath

Inspiration, Mass

Leadership3

Range: touch

Thaumaturgy1, Witchcraft2

Touched object (of max 200kg) levitates for 2 hours. It retains its full mass. This spell can be cast on an armor or weapon, in which case all atheltics requirements would be removed.

Thaumaturgy1, Witchcraft1

Mage Armor, Ultimate

Thaumaturgy6

Mage Flight

Thaumaturgy4, Witchcraft4

Mend

Thaumaturgy1

Message

Thaumaturgy1, Witchcraft1

Subject within line of sight and 100 meters can hear a short message that you whisper silently.


Mind Link

Thaumaturgy3, Witchcraft2

You create a telepathic mind link to a close friend or relative. The two of you can communicate telepathically as long as you concentrate.


Modify Athryss

Ritual: 5 minutes

Thaumaturgy3, Witchcraft3

You can enchant an infant Athryss to grow into the species of Athryss you specify.


Mold Mineral

Ritual: 2 minutes

Thaumaturgy4

You can mold a single metal or stone object (max 10kg) with your hands (not by tools). The object is not heated by this process and it remains moldable by you for 1 hour.


Nails

Ritual: 5 minutes

Thaumaturgy1, Witchcraft2

You enchant 10 nails. The nails can be made out of metal, stone, bone or hardwood. They become at least twice as durable as they were. They can be pushed into any kind of non-magical wood with the bare hands. They will not come out again without force. They will never rust, corrode or rot.


Paralyze Animal

Thaumaturgy3, Witchcraft1

The animal subject is paralyzed until it succeeds a WIL Test (made at the beginning of its turn each round).


Paralyze Monster

Thaumaturgy6, Witchcraft4

The monstrous subject is paralyzed until it succeeds a WIL Test (made at the beginning of the subjects turn each round).


Paralyze Person

Thaumaturgy5, Witchcraft3

The humanoid subject is paralyzed until it succeeds a WIL Test (made at the beginning of its turn each round).


Prestidigitation

Thaumaturgy1, Witchcraft1

Minor magical tricks and illusions.


Purify

Witchcraft1

You purify food or water, making it potable.


Purify, Area

Ritual: 1 hour

Witchcraft3

The soil and flora within 200 squares of you is healed and cured of any natural diseases.


Revivify

Witchcraft4

Touched subject who has died within the last minute is brought back to life with 1 HP.


Search

Ritual: 10 minutes

Thaumaturgy2, Witchcraft2

You get a single yes/no answer to a question about the location of a specific person, creature or object you are thinking of.

You use this ritual six times to narrow down the location of the person you are tracking:

  • Is my target in the same country as me? Yes.
  • Is my target in the same city as me? No.
  • Is my target in the capital? Yes.
  • Is my target in the northern half of the capital ? Yes.
  • Is my target in the market place of the capital? No.
  • Is my target in the butcher’s district of the capital?. Yes.

Sleep

Witchcraft1

Slow

Thaumaturgy3

You extract the pure metal (not just ore, but actual metal) from dirt, stone and water that contain trace metals or ore. Up to 1kg of metal can be extracted.
