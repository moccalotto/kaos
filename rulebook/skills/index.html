Ruleboobook Races Skills Equipment Powers

Skills

Base: The score that you get for free.

Max: The character cannot advance their Skill Score beyond the Max Score in any way.


Acrobatics

Base: AGI ÷ 2, Max: STR + AGI
Climb, jump, tumble and stunts.

Appraisal

Base: PER ÷ 2, Max: PER · 2
Estimate price and authenticity of artwork, gems, coins, weapons, armor, buildings, etc.

Athletics

Base: STR ÷ 2, Max: TOU + WIL
Run, march, swim, endure physical stress.

Battle

Base: 0, Max: STR + PER
Use Battle powers. You cannot use Battle powers if you have not met the Athletics requirements of any armor you are wearing.

Creature Lore

Base: 0, Max: PER · 2
Guestimate an approximation of a key knowledge such as Movement Rate, Defense Points, Hit Points, an Attribute Score, Skill Score, etc.

Deception

Base: CHA ÷ 2, Max: PER + CHA
Disguise, bluff, lie, impersonate.

Geography

Base: PER ÷ 2, Max: PER · 2
Travel routes, local area knowledge, imports/exports, local prices, local laws.

Healing

Base: PER ÷ 2, Max: PER · 2
First Aid, identify diseases and poisons. You can heal 1d6 HP on a successful Healing check. A creature can only receive one such healing per day.

History

Base: PER ÷ 2, Max: PER · 2
Local lore, ancient lore. Knowledge of the old gods, of ancient cultures, races heroes and items.

Languages

Base: 0, Max: PER + CHA
Speak score ÷ 10 langauges in addition to your mother tongue.

Leadership

Base: 0, Max: WIL + CHA
Use Leadership powers.

Literacy

Base: 0, Max: PER · 2
Read and write score ÷ 10 languages.

Melee Combat

Base: AGI ÷ 2, Max: STR + AGI
Attack with a melee weapon or hand-to-hand.

Negotiation

Base: CHA ÷ 2, Max: PER + CHA
Intimidation, haggling, interrogation.

Ranged Combat

Base: PER ÷ 2, Max: AGI + PER
Attack with a missile- or thrown weapon.

Riding

Base: WIL ÷ 2, Max: WIL + CHA
Horses, stags, griffons.

Stealth

Base: AGI ÷ 2, Max: AGI · 2
Hide, sneak, camouflage.

Survival

Base: PER ÷ 2, Max: PER + WIL
Hunting, gathering, direction sense.

Thaumaturgy

Base: 0, Max: PER · 2
Cast Thaumaturgy spells. You cannot cast spells if you have not met the Athletics requirements of any armor you are wearing. You cannot cast spells when wearing a shield. If you are wearing armor, you must succeed an Athletics Test every time you try and cast a spell.

Thievery

Base: AGI ÷ 2, Max: AGI + PER
Pick locks, slight of hand.

Witchcraft

Base: 0, Max: WIL + CHA
Cast Witchcraft spells. You cannot cast spells if you have not met the Athletics requirements of any armor you are wearing. You cannot cast spells when wearing a shield. If you are wearing armor, you must succeed an Athletics Test every time you try and cast a spell.

[Craft]

Base: PER ÷ 2, Max: PER + WIL
[Craft] can be a craft, art or science such as alchemy, blacksmithing, carpentry, engineering, gambling, masonry or painting.
