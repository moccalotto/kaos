KAOS

KAOS is a system of lightweight roleplaying game guidelines. It is meant for experienced and creative gamers, who can infer all the stuff not written here, and who have the confidence to use a system that does not have rules for every concievable scenario.

Round fractions down, modify the rules to yor needs, use common sense, and Have Fun!.
