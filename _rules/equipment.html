Armors

DS: The Defense Score of the armor. This is the percentile chance the armor has of negating an incoming attack. Shields increase the character's overall DS.
Athletics: Armors may require you to have a certain score in Athletics - otherwise, your Defense Score and your Movement Rate will be halved. Shields increase the overall Athletics requirement.
A person wearing a Footman's shield and a Chain Shirt will have a Defense Score of 20+5=25 and an Atheltics requirement of 15+5=20.
