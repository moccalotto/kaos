Base: The base score that all characters get for free during character creation. To improve their character further before game beings, they can spend skill points at a one-to-one rate to improve skills. After that, they can purchase skill upgrades via Advancement Points.

Note that, should the character's attribute scores increase, the character's skill scores do NOT increase with it; the base score is only used during character creation.

Max: The character cannot advance their Skill Score higher than the max score. To increase this ceiling, the player must spend Advancement Points to improve their attribute scores.
