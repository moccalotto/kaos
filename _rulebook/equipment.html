Armors

DP: The number of Defense Points you have if wearing this armor. Defense Points are used to deflect an opponent's attacks. Shields increase the number of DPs.
MR: This is how the armor affects your movement rate.
Athletics: Armors may require you to have a certain score in Athletics - otherwise your movement rate is reduced by 2 (in addition to any penalties the armor may already have). and your Movement Rate will be halved. Shields increase the overall Athletics requirement.
Strain: Is the number of additional points of strain damage you take every time you take a Surge Action.
Example 1
A person wearing Hardened Leather and a Light Shield and an AGI of 23 will have a Defense Score of 23รท10+2+1 = 5, and an Athletics requirement of 20+10 = 30. Such a character would take 1d4 + 1 points of strain damage for every surge action taken.
Example 2
A person wearing Chain Mail and a Tower Shield will have a Defense Score of 4+3 = 7, a Movement Rate penalty of 1+2 = 3, and an Athletics requirement of 35+20 = 55. Such a character would take 1d4 + 2 points of strain damage for every surge action taken.
